Swift Savings Spot – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

Swift Savings Spot ("we," "us," or "our") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your data when you visit our website, sign up for updates, or engage with any tools, content, product recommendations, or partner offers made available through our Services.

By using our Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy and to our Terms. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Services.

Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways:

a. Information You Provide

When you interact with our website or subscribe to receive updates, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and ZIP/postal code
  • Shopping preferences or product interests
  • Information voluntarily submitted through forms, surveys, or sign-ups

b. Information We Collect Automatically

When you visit our website or interact with our emails, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Pages visited, products viewed, links clicked, and session duration
  • Email engagement metrics (opens, clicks, unsubscribes)
  • Cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies used for analytics and personalization

c. Information From Third Parties

We may receive information from affiliates, ecommerce partners, advertising networks, and promotional collaborators to help us recommend deals, discounts, and shopping resources relevant to your interests.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website, tools, and user experience
  • Deliver newsletters, product recommendations, deal alerts, and promotional emails
  • Provide personalized content based on your browsing or shopping interests
  • Analyze performance and engagement to optimize our marketing and product features
  • Respond to questions or support requests
  • Prevent fraud, enhance security, and comply with legal requirements

How We Share Information

We may share your information with trusted third parties, including:

a. Marketing & Advertising Partners

We collaborate with partners in ecommerce, retail, technology, lifestyle, home goods, finance, and related categories. These partners may contact you with product recommendations, discounts, or promotional offers that align with your interests.

b. Service Providers

We use third-party vendors for services such as email delivery, website hosting, analytics, advertising technology, and customer support. These providers are required to protect your information and use it only as directed.

c. Legal or Safety Requirements

We may disclose information when required to comply with applicable laws or legal processes, or to protect our rights, users, and property.

We do not sell personal information for direct monetary compensation. However, certain types of data sharing may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" under state privacy laws.

Email and Text Communications

By subscribing or submitting your information through our website, you consent to receive:

  • Email updates, deal alerts, and product recommendations
  • Promotional communications from us and select partners

You may unsubscribe at any time using the link included in any email.

If you opt in to receive text messages, you may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Standard messaging and data rates may apply.

Consent to receive marketing messages is not required to use our website.

We comply with CAN-SPAM, TCPA, and Do-Not-Call guidelines.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, tracking pixels, and similar technologies to:

  • Recognize returning visitors
  • Personalize product recommendations and offers
  • Measure effectiveness of deals, promotions, and partner campaigns

You may manage or disable cookies through your browser settings; however, certain features may not function properly without them.

We also use third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) that collect anonymized usage data and share aggregated insights with us.

Third-Party Links & Offers

Our website and emails may feature links to external ecommerce sites, partner deals, or promotional offers. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third-party websites.

Before submitting information to any third party, we encourage you to review their privacy policy.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no data transmission or storage system is completely secure, and you acknowledge the inherent risks when using online services.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold
  • Request updates or deletion
  • Opt out of data sharing used for targeted advertising
  • Request a portable copy of your information

To submit a request, email us with the subject line "Privacy Request." We may require additional information to verify your identity.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal obligations.

You may request deletion at any time, subject to applicable retention requirements.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has submitted personal data, we will delete it promptly.

International Users

Swift Savings Spot operates within the United States. If you access the Services from outside the U.S., you consent to the transfer and processing of your information under U.S. privacy laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update or modify this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Services indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
